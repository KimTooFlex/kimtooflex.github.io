WinForms UI Controls & Components.

We created KimTools to heal the frustration of fighting a framework that feels stuck in the past. We’ve poured decades of award-winning experience into building a native SVG ecosystem & finally replacing blurry, static bitmaps with a searchable library of 10,000+ scalable icons that breathe life into your interfaces.

By marrying a Tailwind/Web isnpired architecture with high-fidelity rendering, we’ve replaced the exhausting grind of manual styling with the pure joy of low-code creation.
